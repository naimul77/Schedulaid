About SEIS4IUB:

Welcome to Student Enrollment Information System for Independent University, Bangladesh!


Dear Students,

You are prompted to enter your student ID, name, department and major into the system for analytic purposes.
You are also requested to update your information periodically when changes are made to your record.
We hope you find the software to be operated with ease.



To Respected Faculty Members,

Comparative studies can be conducted based on different variables of interest such as departments, schools, courses and more.
Graphs can be generated and tables can be formed to aid in the depiction of current enrollment and prediction of future outcomes.
We solely devote our development efforts to assure the interoperability of the software to reach your expectations.



For Administration,

All administrative members are welcomed to use the software with specific privileges.
Benefits include limited access to students records and graphical report generation software.
We are proud to inform that development of further system components for administrative purposes are upcoming.



SEIS is a beneficial software regarded for the analysis of enrollment and trend forecasting in IUB.
